A cafe shaped like a loop.

Named for the möbius — one continuous surface, no beginning, no end. That's how we think about a good morning.

Our story

We opened on the lower ground of Chatswood Chase because the area deserved another proper cup of coffee — the kind pulled with care, on beans we actually believe in.

The room is simple: warm light, honest materials, and a menu that reads short because everything on it should earn its place. Come for the flat white. Stay for the steak sandwich.

What we're serving.

A short menu, cooked honestly. Coffee is the headline — the rest of the plate is written to live next to it.

No. 01

Coffee

House espresso blend, single origin filter, batch brew and the full milk menu — iced, oat, hot and patient.

No. 02

Brunch

All-day plates: steak & eggs, the Mobius cheeseburger, hash-brown stacks and seasonal specials on the board.

No. 03

Sandwiches

Pressed sourdough, house-roasted fillings, fries on the side. Built for lunch, good enough for 3pm.

No. 04

Pastries

Laminated, layered, pulled warm from the case. Rotating specials from the pastry bench alongside the classics.
